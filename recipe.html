Roasted Cauliflower Recipe

Cauliflower is one of the greatest vegetables to roast, transforming from dry and tasteless to soft, to sweet and juicy with terrific caramelised edges.

It also requires minimal work to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp each salt and pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1 tbsp parsley , roughly chopped, for sprinkling (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C/425°F (200°C fan).
  2. Toss cauliflower in a large bowl with oil, salt, pepper and garlic if using.
  3. Spread on tray, and roast for 20 minutes.
  4. Flip cauliflower, roast a further 5 minutes until you have golden edges and it’s soft all the way through.
  5. Serve immediately, sprinkled with parsley if using.
